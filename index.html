Privacy Policy

This Text Converter application is designed with your privacy in mind.

Data Storage

All of your profiles are stored exclusively in cookies on your local device. It is not transmitted or stored on any external servers or databases.

Profiles

You can save up to 5 profiles with your preferred text conversion settings. These profiles are stored as cookies on your device.

Cookie Usage

Cookies are used solely to remember your preferences and profiles. If you clear your browser cookies or history, your saved profiles will be erased.

Import/Export

You can export your profiles to JSON files for backup or sharing purposes. When importing profiles, you have control over how conflicts with existing profiles are handled.

By using this application, you acknowledge and agree to this privacy policy.
